About Me

Hi, I'm Jerry! Welcome to my life adventures. From traveling the world to exploring new cultures, I'm here to share my journey with you. I went through a lot in life, from a tough divorce to living on the streets as a kid. Despite the challenges, I strive to be a better person every day and inspire others through my experiences.

My Journey

I have traveled to many places, including Finland, Sweden, Thailand, Singapore, and more. Each trip has brought new experiences and stories that I'm excited to share with you.

My Interests

My Goals

My goals include achieving financial freedom by my 50s, owning a hotel or hostel, and spending more time with my family and kids. I also want to help people get back on their feet after tough times, such as going through a divorce, and inspire others to be better every day.
